Chimera-SDK is a bare-metal development platform for ASICs based on the `Chimera architecture <https://github.com/pulp-platform/chimera>`_, a microcontroller SoC for multi-cluster, heterogeneous systems.

Chimera and Chimera-SDK are developed as part of the `PULP project <https://pulp-platform.org/index.html>`_, a joint effort between ETH Zurich and the University of Bologna.

.. warning::

   Chimera-SDK is currently in an early development stage and this documentation is a work in progress.
